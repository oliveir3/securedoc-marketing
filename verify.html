Verify Documents

Every document signed through SecureDoc includes a blockchain hash and a secure verification link. Users can visit securedoc-verify to check a file’s authenticity.

Our system uses:

  • Document hashing via SHA-256
  • Immutable proof via Hedera topic messages
  • Timestamp + signer identity
  • Public-facing verification portal
